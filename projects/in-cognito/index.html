In-Cognito

29 October 2025

In-cognito was a live performance, AV event and Halloween Party.

    party exhibition live performance

InTouch

Artists played with the idea of being in disguise, obscured, hidden or acting in an alter-ego. Work touched on topics including RPGs, surveillance and technical infrastructures. Artists shared music, performances, AV sets and film work. Halloween costumes were encouraged. Invited guests were trampbunny, Irini Kalaitzidi and Something About The Worm.
