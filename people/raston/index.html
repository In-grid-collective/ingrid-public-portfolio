Rebecca Aston

Website: https://rebeccaaston.com/

Rebecca Aston is an artist and creative technologist working with a range of time-based media and computation. She works with paint, textiles and creates interactive software art using different programming languages and hardware. Trained as a painter, she is interested in vision and embodied perception, looking at how our bodies exist within the loop of technological systems. She has a BA in Fine Art from Yale and an MFA in Computational Art from Goldsmiths UoL. She is a lecturer in the computing department at Goldsmiths UoL.
